## 题目 1：Walk me through your resume.

**面试官**: Good morning, thanks for coming in. Let's start — walk me through your resume.

**候选人**: Sure. I'm a third-year Finance student at Queen's University. I've had two relevant experiences — a summer at a boutique advisory firm where I built financial models, and a part-time role at my university's student investment fund where I managed a $50K portfolio.

**面试官**: Tell me more about the financial modeling work.

**候选人**: I built DCF and comps models for three client engagements. One was a $20M acquisition in the healthcare space where my analysis was presented to the client's board.

### 评分：(4/5)

- Structure: [GOOD] Concise, chronological, hits key points
- Relevance: [GOOD] Highlighted finance-specific experience
- Depth: [GOOD] Good detail on modeling work
- Improvement: Could mention what drew you to capital markets specifically

## 题目 2：What's happening in the bond market right now?

**面试官**: Let's talk markets. What's your take on the current bond market environment?

**候选人**: Um, interest rates have been... I think the Bank of Canada has been cutting rates recently? Bond prices should be going up because of that.

**面试官**: Can you be more specific about where rates are and what that means for RBC's capital markets business?
**候选人**: I'm not sure of the exact numbers right now...

### 评分：(2/5)

- Market knowledge: [WEAK] Vague — "I think" and "recently" show lack of preparation
- Depth: [WEAK] No specific numbers (rates, spreads, recent moves)
- Relevance: [OK] Correct direction (rates down → bonds up) but no specifics

**改进建议：** 面试前一天必须check: Bank of Canada利率、10Y bond yield、recent market moves、1-2 recent deals。能说出具体数字（如"BoC rate is at 3.25%, down from 5% peak"）会大幅加分。
