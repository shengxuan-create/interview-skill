# RBC Financial Analyst — 面试题库

> 共18题：Behavioral 6 + Technical/Market 6 + Case 4 + Situational 2
> 无LeetCode/coding题 — 银行分析师面试以behavioral和市场知识为主

---

## Behavioral Questions（银行文化导向）

1. **Walk me through your resume.**
   - 考察点：communication, narrative, fit
   - 银行偏好：简洁有条理，重点highlight finance相关经历
   - 时间控制：2分钟内

2. **Why banking? Why RBC specifically?**
   - 考察点：motivation, industry knowledge, company research
   - 参考思路：genuine interest in capital markets + RBC specific advantages（加拿大最大、全球presence）

3. **Tell me about a time you worked in a high-pressure, deadline-driven environment.**
   - 考察点：stress management, reliability
   - 银行偏好：banking hours are long — 展示你能handle pressure
   - 参考思路：STAR框架，强调quality under pressure

4. **Describe a time you identified an error in your work. How did you handle it?**
   - 考察点：attention to detail, integrity, accountability
   - 银行偏好：合规文化——发现错误主动上报比隐瞒重要100倍

5. **Tell me about a team project where you had to rely on others to deliver.**
   - 考察点：teamwork, trust, delegation
   - 参考思路：银行面试非常重视team fit

6. **Where do you see yourself in 5 years?**
   - 考察点：career planning, commitment to finance
   - 参考思路：show a realistic path within RBC/banking

## Technical / Market Knowledge Questions

7. **What's happening in the bond market right now? How are interest rates affecting capital markets?**
   - 考察点：market awareness, macro understanding
   - 准备：面试前一天看Bloomberg/Reuters，知道当前利率水平和趋势

8. **Walk me through a DCF (Discounted Cash Flow) analysis.**
   - 考察点：valuation fundamentals
   - 关键步骤：project FCF → determine WACC → discount → terminal value → enterprise value

9. **What's the difference between equity and debt financing? When would a company prefer one over the other?**
   - 考察点：corporate finance basics
   - 参考思路：cost of capital, dilution, tax shield, flexibility

10. **If interest rates rise, what happens to bond prices? Explain why.**
    - 考察点：fixed income fundamentals
    - 答案：inverse relationship — higher rates → higher discount rate → lower present value

11. **Tell me about a recent M&A deal or IPO that caught your attention.**
    - 考察点：market engagement, analytical thinking
    - 准备：pick 1-2 recent deals，能说出rationale、valuation、market reaction

12. **How would you build a comparable company analysis (comps)?**
    - 考察点：valuation methodology
    - 步骤：select peers → gather metrics → calculate multiples → apply to target

## Case / Group Exercise Questions

13. **Case: A manufacturing company wants to expand into Asia. Evaluate this decision.**
    - 考察点：structured thinking, business judgment
    - 框架：market attractiveness + company capability + financial impact + risks

14. **Case: Should Company X acquire Company Y for $500M?**
    - 考察点：M&A evaluation, financial analysis
    - 框架：strategic fit → synergies → valuation → financing → integration risks

15. **Market sizing: How large is the Canadian mortgage market?**
    - 考察点：estimation skills, market knowledge
    - 方法：top-down (GDP → housing → mortgages) or bottom-up (households × avg mortgage)

16. **Group exercise: Your team has $100M to allocate across 5 investment options. Present your recommendation.**
    - 考察点：teamwork, communication, financial judgment, leadership in group settings

## Situational Questions

17. **You discover an error in a report that's already been sent to a client. What do you do?**
    - 考察点：integrity, risk management, client relationship
    - 银行文化：transparency and immediate escalation is ALWAYS the right answer

18. **A senior colleague asks you to skip a compliance step to meet a deadline. How do you respond?**
    - 考察点：ethics, compliance awareness, professional courage
    - 银行文化：compliance is non-negotiable — politely but firmly refuse
