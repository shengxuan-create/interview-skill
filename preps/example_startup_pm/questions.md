# NovaTech AI PM — 面试题库

> 共15题：Behavioral 5 + Product Case 5 + Data/Technical 3 + Situational 2
> 注意：无公开面经数据，题目基于行业通用PM面试框架生成。

---

## Behavioral Questions

1. **Tell me about a product you took from 0 to 1.**
   - 考察点：ownership, product lifecycle experience
   - 参考思路：强调discovery → definition → delivery全流程

2. **Describe a time you had to make a product decision without enough data.**
   - 考察点：decision-making under uncertainty (critical for startups)
   - 参考思路：展示how you balanced speed vs. thoroughness

3. **Tell me about a time you said no to a feature request from a key stakeholder.**
   - 考察点：prioritization, communication, backbone
   - 参考思路：用framework说明why + 如何manage关系

4. **How do you work with engineers when there's a disagreement on scope?**
   - 考察点：cross-functional collaboration, negotiation
   - 参考思路：seek to understand technical constraints first

5. **Why do you want to join a startup at this stage?**
   - 考察点：motivation, risk tolerance, culture fit
   - 参考思路：genuine reasons + awareness of startup challenges
---

## Product Case Questions

6. **How would you improve our AI customer support product?**
   - 考察点：product sense, customer empathy, analytical thinking
   - 参考思路：先clarify目标用户和pain point → 提出3个方向 → 用impact/effort评估

7. **Design a new feature: AI-powered ticket routing for enterprise support teams.**
   - 考察点：product design, technical understanding, prioritization
   - 参考思路：user flow → requirements → MVP scope → success metrics

8. **Our user activation rate dropped 15% last month. How would you investigate?**
   - 考察点：data analysis, root cause thinking, hypothesis-driven approach
   - 参考思路：funnel analysis → segment by cohort → external vs internal factors

9. **How would you prioritize these 5 features for next quarter?**
   - 考察点：prioritization framework, stakeholder balance
   - 参考思路：RICE or ICE framework → align with company goals → communicate trade-offs

10. **A key enterprise customer is threatening to churn. What do you do?**
    - 考察点：customer management, urgency handling, cross-team coordination
    - 参考思路：immediate triage → root cause → short-term fix + long-term solution

---

## Data / Technical Questions

11. **How would you define success metrics for our product?**
    - 考察点：metrics thinking, north star metric, leading/lagging indicators

12. **Walk me through how you'd set up an A/B test for a new onboarding flow.**
    - 考察点：experimentation, statistical thinking, practical constraints

13. **Explain how a large language model works to a non-technical stakeholder.**
    - 考察点：technical communication, AI knowledge (relevant for AI company)

---

## Situational Questions

14. **You join and realize the product roadmap is mostly driven by the loudest customer. What do you do?**
    - 考察点：strategic thinking, process improvement, diplomacy

15. **Engineering says your top feature will take 3 months. You need it in 6 weeks. How do you handle this?**
    - 考察点：negotiation, scope management, creative problem-solving
