## 题目 2：How would you improve our AI customer support product?

**面试官**: Let's say you're our PM on day 1. How would you approach improving our product?

**候选人**: First, I'd want to understand who our users are and what their main pain points are. Can I ask — who's the primary user?

**面试官**: Support agents at mid-market SaaS companies, 50-500 employees.
**候选人**: Got it. I'd focus on three areas: reducing first-response time with better AI routing, improving agent confidence by showing AI-suggested answers with sources, and building a feedback loop so the AI improves from agent corrections.

**面试官**: How would you prioritize those three?

**候选人**: I'd start with AI-suggested answers because it directly reduces handle time, which is the metric support teams care most about. I'd measure success by average handle time reduction and agent adoption rate.

### 评分：(4/5)

- Product sense: [GOOD] Good framework — user-centric, actionable
- Prioritization: [GOOD] Clear rationale tied to user value
- Metrics: [GOOD] Defined measurable success criteria
- Depth: [OK] Could discuss edge cases and risks
